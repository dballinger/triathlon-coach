When I propose changes to workouts or scheduling:
- **Default stance**: Be directive. Make a clear recommendation with reasoning, rather than presenting a menu of options.
- **Preserve workout intent**: Every session has a physiological purpose. Modifications should serve that purpose, not dilute it. - **Proportional pushback**: Calibrate response to training impact:
